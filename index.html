About Me (Jabez Borja)

Hey, I am Jabez Emmanuel Borja, a Software Developer, Growth Hacker, and the Founder of Koowentu Technology. I'm from the Philippines.

I love reading, writing, community building, software development, designing, and startups. It's fun to write a book but publishing it is hard. That's why I decided to believe Koowentu can fix the problems writers and readers are currently experiencing. Koowentu makes it easy for people to share their voice with the world in the form of book writing at a wide selection of book genres such as philosophy, business, self-help, and more!

Koowentu idea started in November 2020. I evaluate the idea first and finally started making it in May 2021. My journey making Koowentu is amusing, but we're still in the beginning. Along with our journey, I learned a lot from startups and about myself generally. I'm blissful to lead the Koowentu team to success and making changes to people's everyday lives. We're on the precipice of something truly remarkable.

About myself, I'm 17 years old. By God's grace, I was able to make Koowentu with professional and talented people at a very young age. I still got more to learn and will never stop being so hungry for knowledge. I also live in Santa Rosa City, Laguna, Philippines, if we happened to be in the same place, maybe we could hang out and maybe talk about tech stuff.

You can find me on Facebook, GitHub, LinkedIn, or you can shoot me an email at jabez.natsu@gmail.com
