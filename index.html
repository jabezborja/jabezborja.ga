Hey, I am Jabez Emmanuel Borja, a Software Developer, Growth Hacker, and the Founder of Koowentu Technology. I'm from the Philippines. I love reading, writing, community building, software development, designing, strategic thinking, and startups.

"Alone we can do so little; together we can do so much." Collaborating to create books nowadays is still not digitalized, publishing your own book online has so many gotchas, and writing can't be a long-term career yet. That's why I believe Koowentu can fix those problems that writers are currently experiencing. Because we have seen the problems first hand, we're solving it.

Koowentu idea started in November 2020. I evaluate the idea first and finally started making it in May 2021. My journey making Koowentu is amusing, but we're still in the beginning. Along with our journey, I learned a lot from startups and about myself generally. I'm blissful to lead the Koowentu team to success and making changes to people's everyday lives. We're on the precipice of something truly remarkable.

About myself, I'm 17 years old. By God's grace, I was able to find my passion which is communities and software development at the age of 15. I still got more to learn and will never stop being so hungry for knowledge. I also live in Santa Rosa City, Laguna, Philippines, if we happened to be in the same place, maybe we could hang out and maybe talk about tech stuff.

I also started a new blog where I share about my learnings, me personally, and others. Check it out!
